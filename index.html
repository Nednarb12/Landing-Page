The Hidden Treasure of Lithuania

Along the south-eastern coast of the Baltic Sea lies a serene landscape. Many of Lithuanians spend their summers here, but it's beauty is widely unknown to many of us throughout the world, a treasure that is protected by Lithuania's justifiably strict natural conservation.

True Beauty

A visit to "Dutch Hat" will enlighten your primal senses to where our idea of beauty comes from in nature.

According to locals in the area, the pine forests here release small amounts of iodine into the air. This iodine supposedly has positive health benefits, along with the ability to have a darker tan if a sunny day should arrive.

If you are lucky enough to find clear skies in this cloud covered country, and also lucky enough to be staring towards the Baltic horizon, you will have witnessed a truly art-inspiring, magical, cosmic occurance. You will momentarily believe yourself to be in a fantasy.

Ryti akimis
